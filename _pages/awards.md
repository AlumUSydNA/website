- 2005 - Gerard Bassell (MBBS '73)

- 1998 - Prof Enid Gilbert-Barness AO (MBBS '50, MD '83, MD '99)
- 1997 - Dr Clifford Kwan-Gett (BSc '55, BE Mech & Elect '57, MBBS '64)
